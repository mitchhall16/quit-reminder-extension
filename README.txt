QUIT REMINDER - Chrome Extension
=================================

Helps you stay accountable when quitting nicotine and alcohol.

FEATURES:
- Every new tab requires typing your affirmation before browsing
- Timed reminders every 40 minutes (configurable)
- Tracks days clean, affirmations typed, and money saved
- Editable affirmation text
- Mood check-ins with the reminders

INSTALLATION:
1. First, generate the icon:
   - Open generate-icon.html in Chrome
   - Right-click the image and "Save image as..." icon.png
   - Save it in this folder

2. Load the extension:
   - Open Chrome and go to: chrome://extensions
   - Turn ON "Developer mode" (top right toggle)
   - Click "Load unpacked"
   - Select this folder (quit-reminder-extension)

3. Configure:
   - Click the extension icon in Chrome toolbar
   - Edit your affirmation text
   - Set reminder interval (default 40 min)
   - Set your daily spending (for savings calculator)
   - Click "Save Settings"

HOW IT WORKS:
- Every new tab shows your affirmation - you must type it to continue
- Every 40 minutes, a reminder tab opens with an ALARM SOUND
- You must type your affirmation to dismiss the reminder
- Your progress is saved and shown (days clean, money saved)

CUSTOMIZE YOUR AFFIRMATION:
Click the extension icon and edit the text to whatever helps you most.

Examples:
- "I don't need to drink. I don't need nicotine. I am stronger than my cravings."
- "This craving will pass. I am in control."
- "I choose health. I choose freedom. I choose myself."

Good luck. You got this.
