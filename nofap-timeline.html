The Science Behind It

Pornography floods your brain with dopamine - the same chemical released by drugs. Over time, your brain builds tolerance, requiring more extreme content for the same hit. This rewires your reward system.

When you stop, your brain goes through withdrawal as dopamine receptors heal. This is why the first 2 weeks are hardest. But stick with it - neuroplasticity means your brain CAN rewire back to normal.

Studies show it takes roughly 90 days for significant changes in brain structure. That's why 90 days is the standard "reboot" period.
